# zomato-data-analysis
PROJECT OVERVIEW

This project performs an in-depth Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on the Zomato restaurant dataset to understand customer preferences, restaurant performance, and ordering behavior.
The analysis focuses on identifying key patterns related to restaurant types, ratings, votes, pricing, and online vs offline ordering modes to derive actionable business insights.

OBJECTIVES

Analyze customer engagement across different restaurant types

Study the relationship between restaurant ratings and customer votes

Identify rating trends across the platform

Examine customer spending behavior, particularly for couples

Compare customer satisfaction between online and offline ordering modes

Provide data-driven recommendations to improve business performance

DATA SET

The dataset contains structured information on restaurants listed on Zomato, including:

Restaurant type

Customer ratings

Number of votes

Online ordering availability

Cost for two people

Location details

The dataset was cleaned and preprocessed to ensure accuracy and consistency during analysis.

TOOLS AND TECHNOLOGIES

Python

Pandas – Data manipulation and preprocessing

NumPy – Numerical computations

Matplotlib & Seaborn – Data visualization

Jupyter Notebook

ANALYSIS PERFORMED

Data cleaning and handling of missing values

Conversion of rating values into numerical format

Aggregation and grouping by restaurant type

Exploratory data analysis using statistical methods

Visualization of insights using charts and plots

KEY INSIGHTA

Casual Dining and Quick Bites restaurants receive the highest customer engagement in terms of votes.

Most restaurants have ratings between 3.5 and 4.2, indicating generally good service quality.

Restaurants offering online ordering receive higher average ratings and customer engagement.

Couples ordering food online tend to spend a moderate amount per order, reflecting value-oriented behavior.

Offline orders are highest for Casual Dining restaurants, highlighting an opportunity for targeted promotional offers.

BUSINESS RECOMENDATIONS

Focus marketing and promotional campaigns on Casual Dining and Quick Bites restaurants.

Introduce incentives to convert offline customers to online ordering.

Emphasize affordability and convenience to improve customer satisfaction and ratings.

CONCLUSION

The analysis highlights that customer convenience, reasonable pricing, and consistent service quality are key factors influencing restaurant success on Zomato.
This project demonstrates practical skills in data analysis, visualization, and business insight generation, making it suitable for inclusion in a professional data analytics portfolio.
